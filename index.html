The problem we're solving

Thalassemia patients depend on lifelong transfusions to survive

Yet the systems meant to keep blood flowing to them remain scattered and manual.

🩸

Lifelong need

Thalassemia patients often need a blood transfusion every 2–4 weeks — not once, but for life.

🏥

Scattered systems

Hospitals and donors have no shared platform to connect quickly and reliably when blood is needed.

📉

Donor drop-off

Willing donors exist, but there's little to track, guide, or motivate them toward repeat donation.

How it works

From sign-up to saving a life, in four steps

Register & verify

Create an account with your CNIC and documents. Your identity is verified before your first donation.

Choose a hospital

Pick any registered partner hospital that's convenient and book a donation visit.

Donate blood

Visit the hospital; staff verify your documents and complete the donation safely.

Track progress

The donation is logged to your profile automatically — building toward your next reward.

Reward system

Give 4 times, get rewarded

Every donation fills one drop. Complete four, and you unlock a free full body test at any partner hospital — a genuine health benefit that turns a one-time good deed into a lasting habit.

🎁
Free body test unlocked at every 4th donation — and hospitals stay stocked for patients in ongoing need.
Two dedicated portals

One platform. Two portals. One life-saving mission.

Donor Portal

  • Simple sign-up with CNIC & document verification
  • Secure login to a personal dashboard
  • Find and book any partner hospital
  • Track total donations & 90-day eligibility
  • Get notified when your free test unlocks

Hospital Portal

  • Register with licence & blood-bank documents
  • Secure login for hospital staff
  • Verify incoming donors & record donations
  • Live blood availability by group
  • Broadcast urgent Thalassemia requests
Our core mission

Built around Thalassemia patients

Thalassemia is a genetic blood disorder in which patients — often from childhood — need regular, lifelong transfusions to stay alive. Every donor sign-up on Life Drop Saver directly supports the hospitals that treat them.

"Every pint donated through this platform is a step toward a Thalassemia patient's next transfusion."
